A lot Of Activity Can Be Joining In Desaru

Desaru offers a wide range of activities for visitors to enjoy. From relaxing on the pristine beaches and swimming in the crystal-clear waters to engaging in thrilling water sports like jet skiing and kayaking, there's something for everyone. You can also explore the local marine life with snorkeling and diving excursions or enjoy a leisurely boat ride. For those interested in land activities, Desaru boasts scenic nature trails, a vibrant fruit farm, and a fascinating ostrich farm. The Desaru Coast Adventure Waterpark provides hours of fun for families, while the Desaru Firefly Valley offers a magical evening experience. Whether you're looking for adventure, relaxation, or family-friendly fun, Desaru has it all.

Snorkeling

Snorkeling is an option that few exercise as there are no colorful fish or brilliant corals to view. The water is not deep enough for scuba diving though.

Surfing

The wind makes it possible to go surfing with the participants overjoyed at the prospect of skirting the waves and enjoying the experience due to the total lack of jellyfish and sharks infesting the water.

fireflies

As night descends upon the Sungai Lebam mangrove forest, a captivating sight unfolds: the mesmerizing dance of fireflies. These magical insects emit radiant hues of yellow, green, and pale red, gathering around the Berembang trees in this expansive mangrove swamp. Hundreds of fireflies flickering together create an unforgettable natural light show.
