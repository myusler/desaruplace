LOTUS DESARU BEACH RESORT & SPA

Malaysia’s Largest Beach Resort. Lotus Desaru Beach Resort & Spa, is a popular beach resort and a major tourist destination in Malaysia, located in the coastal area of the southern region in the state of Johor, Malaysia. Lotus Desaru Beach Resort & Spa, ideally designed to accommodate family-oriented holiday makers. The resort is set within a sprawling 25 acres, inclusive of 18 blocks, comprising 793 apartments, suites & penthouses. Welcome to Lotus Desaru Beach Resort & Spa, 'The resort for all reasons' surrounded with water, this internationally known beach is the most popular beach along the east-coast in the district of Kota Tinggi, has a stretch of about 26 km of pristine, sandy beach and fringed with palm trees. «Desaru» means «Village Of Casuarinas» or palms. The malay word «Desa» which means village and the word «Ru» is the local name for Casuarinas. Desaru Golden Beach Resort

The Desaru Golden Beach Hotel is set amidst lush tropical greenery on the idyllic beachfront of the south east coast of Johor, Malaysia. Once you walk into the resort beach front you can immediately guess how the resort gets its name. This resort offer two type of accommodation, villas and hotel rooms. The rooms was cozy and provided with hot shower, tv, telephone, coffee & tea making facilities and air-condition. From the beautiful seaside villas to the diverse outdoors sports such as jungle trekking, 4x4 off-road, driving, mountain biking and the numerous beach activities including windsurfing and jet skis, a perfect blend of refreshing holiday is awaiting you. A wide aray of fresh seafood awaits at the Chinese Seafood Restaurants which overlooks the sea. You can enjoy a cocktail and dance at the cosy Blue Horizon Lounge or sit and relax at Sunrise Coffee House. This resort can cater business functions or seminars which can accommodate up to 400 people.

Sebana Cove Resort

Escape to the serene town of Pengerang and indulge in a peaceful getaway at Sebana Cove Resort. Surrounded by lush greenery and stunning beaches, this tranquil retreat offers spacious and clean rooms equipped with air conditioning, complimentary WiFi, and refreshing showers. Enjoy the convenience of a fully-equipped kitchen and unwind by the stunning pool or in the relaxing sauna. Take in the breathtaking views of the marina and sunset as you savor fresh seafood in the local fishing village. With exceptional staff service and a tranquil atmosphere, Sebana Cove Resort is the perfect choice for two travelers seeking a peaceful escape.
