A Lot Of Eating Place

Medan Selera Desa Food Court

(Desaru Holiday Chalets, next to public beach). Simple restaurant with a few outdoor stalls (hence "food court") serving up Malay basics like fried rice and Ramly burgers. Simple, tasty and cheap.

Terminal Cafe

(Tanjung Belungkor Ferry Terminal). Another simple, friendly little eatery dishing out Malay favorites, located in the ferry terminal building (look for the signs to "Canteen"). The nasi ayam (chicken rice) is excellent.

Riverside

Adjacent to the thrilling Desaru Adventure Waterpark.Choose from a whole list of food to satisfy your tastebuds from local and international delicacies.
