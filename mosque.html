MASJID AL SYIFA' BANDAR PENAWAR

The Bandar Penawar Mosque was inaugurated on 18th Ramadan 1414, which corresponds to 1st March 1994 AD. This mosque was built specifically as a place of worship, such as for prayer and seclusion (Iktikaf). This building also serves as a center for culture, economic activities, medicine, and the development of Islamic outreach (Dakwah), as well as a center for Muslim community activities.
